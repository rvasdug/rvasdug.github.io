### Description
This is a casual event for software developers of all experience levels to hang out, hack on side projects, and meet others in the industry. There will be no prepared talks or demos.

**Format**

* We do introductions at 6:15 pm sharp!
* Optional: Bring your laptop.
* Work on anything you want: open-source, closed-source, side-projects, work, play.
* Or simply socialize and network.
* All experience levels and technologies are welcome.
