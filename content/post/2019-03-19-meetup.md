### Description
So much of the emphasis in IoT is put on the the "Things" in IoT, but not much goes into understanding the "Internet' side of the equation. Fundamentally though, it's probably the tougher nut to crack when building out IoT solutions. Come get a primer on how IoT solutions work end to end and see a couple of of cool IoT demos that will really drive home the point.

### Speaker: Blaize Stewart
[Blaize](https://www.blaize.net/) is a senior consultant with Wintellect and a Microsoft Azure MVP, Microsoft Certified Trainer, Partner Cloud Solution Architect with Microsoft. Blaize has been interested in software development since childhood. He wrote his first commercial app while still in high school. Since that time, Blaize has had diverse experience in all things IT ranging from a network administrator to a software architect and everything in between.
