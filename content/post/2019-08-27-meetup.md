### Description
This month will be our first ever evening of lightning talks. What is a lightning talk? A lightning talk is a very short presentation lasting only a few minutes, in this case about 15 minutes. It gives us a chance to talk about a variety of subjects and meet some more of the great folks here in our community.

Line up:
* Scott Wellman - No Or Low-Code PaaS Systems
* Viji Natarajan - The Business Case For DevOps
* Hakon Jonsson - Domain Driven Design in NestJS
* Michael Blakeney - Site Reliability Engineering Availability
* Marcus Wood - JAM in 15 (Javascript APIs Markup)
* Alfonso Guerra - Design Patterns
* Andrew Miller - Developing Cross-Platform Applications

See you there!
