### Description
Please note: This is a week later than we normally meet. We usually met the 3rd Tuesday of the month, this time we will meet on the 4th Tuesday.

We have a special treat this month - our first ever user group social/happy hour. It will held at Bar Louies on Broad Street (beside Short Pump Town Center). Unfortunately we cannot accept guests for this particular meetup. The first 30 people to RSVP and attend will receive 2 drink tickets good for the beverage of your choice (legal age corresponding to your choice please).

The user group will provide some food options for the happy hour.

This is a limited RSVP event so please, if you RSVP but your plans change and you cannot make it, please update your RSVP so someone else can attend. Thanks! This is gonna be fun so I hope to see you there!

Cheers!

-Kevin
