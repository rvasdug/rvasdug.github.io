### Description
Azure provides many different services that can serve as the "glue" for applications you want to deploy to the cloud. In this talk, we will look at no-code solutions like Logic Apps, routing solutions like Service Bus and messaging solutions like Storage Queues to connect the pieces of our applications together. No experience necessary.

### Speaker: Sidney Andrews
Sidney Andrews is an Azure MVP and Microsoft Certified Trainer with SeeSharpRun.NET. He has a background in [ASP.NET](http://asp.net/) web development along with extensive experience developing applications using XAML. Sidney has driven efforts to develop and deliver Azure readiness training through channels such as the Ignite Conference, Microsoft Virtual Academy, Microsoft Official Courseware, internal Microsoft training and even public whitepapers.
