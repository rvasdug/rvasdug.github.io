### Description
Whether you are creating a desktop application, native mobile app, or a web application, interacting with data dependencies such as REST APIs introduces interesting challenges and hardships. Do you find yourself struggling to creating useful test data? Frustrated when services start failing, interrupting your work? Do you try to get creative to avoid charges associated with calling third-party services during development and testing? We are excited to share what we’ve learned working with open source tools that solve these commons hardships, and have resulted in a measurable acceleration in our development. Come learn how you can mock your dependencies and delight your development team.
