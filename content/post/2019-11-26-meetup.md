### Description
There will be some code examples, but the focus will be more about “how you think about” the code.

This presentation will contain a lot of Q&A to find out what the audience is facing in their own work environments, and will attempt to adjust the focus of the conversation to accommodate their needs and interests. Please expect the majority of time to be spent in the last one or two bullets …

* Background
* Enterprise architecture and DevOps - what it means to developers
* Designing to the organization
* Development objectives
* Relationship to the organization
* Accommodating change, ensuring stability
* Patterns
* Fundamentals to never forget
* Ins and Outs of code designs
* Abstraction - making code great

We look forward to seeing everyone there....

### Speaker: Armin Burkholder
Armin has more years of experience in IT than he cares to admit. He has served most sectors of industry: financial, manufacturing, state and federal government, public utility, education, and non-profit. He is primarily an application developer, supplemented with roles as technical manager, team lead, analyst, project manager, application architect, technical designer, consultant, advisor and mentor. He has developed and taught technical curricula for college, and taught product certification courses. His experiences give him a unique perspective on the subject of application development.
