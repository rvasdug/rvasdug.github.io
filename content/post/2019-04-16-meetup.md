### Description
Interested in mobile app development? Kick-start your learning by creating knowledgeable building blocks for React Native, a cross-platform JavaScript framework developed by Facebook. Let's see how well React Native transforms your sketches and wire frames into a full blown iOS and Android mobile apps.

We look forward to seeing everyone there.

### Speaker: Jay Soumphontphakdy
Jay is a Cross-Platform Mobile App developer who enjoys working in a trans-disciplinary manner— collaborating and wearing hats between roles for design, development, and QA. He is dedicated to continuing his education to create high quality user friendly applications, recently becoming an ICAgile Certified Professional in Agile Testing and Test Automation.
