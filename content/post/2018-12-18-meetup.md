### Description
It is time for our 2018 Holiday Happy Hour! It will be held at Capital Ale House in Innsbrook (corner of Broad Street and Cox Rd). Unfortunately we cannot accept guests for this meetup. The first 25 people to RSVP and attend will receive 1 drink ticket good for the beverage of your choice (legal age corresponding to your choice please).

This is a limited RSVP event so please, if you RSVP but your plans change and you cannot make it, please update your RSVP so someone else can attend.

This is an event focused on our technical members so we ask that non-technical members allow our technical members first chance at RSVP. Thanks! This is gonna be fun so I hope to see you there!

Happy Holidays!
